About Hackathon

Welcome to the Technex Hackathon

Technex - Hackathon is the first version of University Hackathon in Nagpur under Technex, the Annual Technical Fest of St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, Nagpur. It is a platform where hackers, developers, innovators, entreuprenuers, students can come together to create solutions to real life problems. Developers can showcase their abilities, students can increase their network, innovators can create new solutions. We invite all the young minds out there to come and showcase their skills, increase their network and have fun.

Technex Hackathon serves as a platform to encourage enthusiastic minds to brainstorm on solutions for challenging issues from all over India. The unified motive of gathering all like minded hackers to our alma mater, is what led to the creation of the idea in St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, Nagpur, the first of its kind. We'll provide a platform for innovations, where developers can test and showcase their potential to the best of their abilities. The hackathon will also have keynotes & workshops from executives from the industry.

Please refer FAQ section for any queries.

About College

St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, Nagpur

St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering & Technology, Nagpur is one of the premier private institutions providing engineering courses in Nagpur. It was founded on the belief of proving quality education. It has completed 10 successful year of providing quality education to the students. Student from all over the state come here to study and bring out the best in them.

We are pleased to inform you that our college is premier Engineering Institute, rendering quality technical education in Central India. It is run by the Nagpur Pallotine Society, part of the International Pallottine Society having its presence in 52 countries . The college is affiliated to Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Maharashtra and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The college has a strength ofover 2000 students from different parts of the country; is proactive in the areas of innovation & amp; research, hosting several events to encourage students. We are actively associated with e Yantra competitions, IIT Bombay and in 2016 have set up the e Yantra lab under e LSI, a collaborative initiative of IIT Bombay and MHRD.
